Welcome to Our page for save our streets

Hello Ladies and Gentlmen for visitin our page which is brought to you by S.O.S otherwise known as Save Our Streets. This page will briefly tell you about what it is we do for the communities with the highest acts of violence.We are located in places like Brooklyn NY, and the Bronx NY. We want to stop the acts of violence in the black communities. We want to speak to the youth and try to get them on track. We like to do for the community all we ask is for them to love like a community.

The things we do

  1. Go around talking to the youth
  2. Give out shirts, hats and bands
  3. Support the youths summer basketball games
  4. Do barbeques
  5. Support the community with things like public health etc...
